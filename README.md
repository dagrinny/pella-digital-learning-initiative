# Pella Digital Learning Initiative (PDLI)

## Overview
A 24-month community digital literacy programme delivering foundational 
computer education to 800+ rural students in Pella, Hong Local Government 
Area, Adamawa State, Nigeria.

## The Problem
Over 3,980 students in Pella have never accessed a computer. No ICT 
facility exists in any of the three government schools. Digital exclusion 
is threatening their academic and economic futures.

## Our Solution
- Solar-powered computer lab with 25 workstations
- Training for 12 local teachers in ICT pedagogy
- Structured bilingual (English/Hausa) digital literacy curriculum
- Community-led sustainability model

## Key Facts
- Grant requested: USD $120,000
- Direct beneficiaries: 800 students + 200 adult community members
- Duration: 24 months
